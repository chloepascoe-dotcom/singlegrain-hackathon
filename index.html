The Rules

1

Everyone Ships Something

You either own a solo project OR are an equal contributor to a group project. No watching someone else demo their thing. If you're in a group, you must be able to explain what YOU built or contributed.

2

Client-Facing or Client-Impacting

Every project must be either shippable to clients (tool, workflow, deliverable) or demonstrably improves client accounts (with proof). Ask yourself: "Can I show this to a client and they'd be impressed?"

3

No Reporting Projects

Find other ways to add value this hackathon.

4

Progress > Perfection

A working project with expansion and refinement opportunities beats sitting on a project until it's perfect. Ship when beneficial.

SEO Projects

AI

Tiger Claw - SEO AI Brain

The central SEO AI system that all projects below feed into. Tiger Claw is the unified brain that powers SEO intelligence, with each project below serving as specialized arms/capabilities.

Arms feeding into Tiger Claw:

1

AI Content Brief Generator

Build a tool that takes a target keyword + competitor URLs and outputs a full content brief (outline, word count, headers, semantic keywords, intent) ready to hand to a writer or client.

2

Technical SEO Audit Workflow

Create an AI-assisted workflow that crawls a client URL, identifies top technical issues, and generates a client-ready prioritized fix list with explanations they can actually understand.

3

Competitor Gap Analyzer

Tool that compares a client's content/keyword footprint against 2-3 competitors and surfaces the highest-value gaps as actionable recommendations.
